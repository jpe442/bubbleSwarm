bubbleSwarm

Thank you for playing bubbleSwarm! The goal of the game is pretty simple. Get as many points as you can in the time allowed. There is a minimum amount of points required to advance to the next stage.

TIP: Sometimes bubbles get "entangled" by one another, it is often a good strategy to pop those bubbles before they get free.

A "vanilla" JavaScript game by Josh Earlenbaugh, PhD
